Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical detailing service take?

A typical detailing session can take anywhere from 1.5 to 5 hours, depending on the service package you select and the condition of your vehicle.

Do I need to provide water or electricity for mobile services?

No, our mobile detailing unit is fully equipped with all necessary supplies, including water and power.

How often should I have my vehicle detailed?

We recommend a full detailing service every 4 to 6 months to keep your vehicle in pristine condition, with maintenance washes every two weeks.

Can you remove all stains and scratches from my vehicle?

While we strive to remove as many imperfections as possible, some stains and scratches may be too deep or permanent to be fully removed. However, we can significantly improve the appearance of your vehicle.

Are the cleaning products you use eco-friendly?

Absolutely! We use high-quality, eco-friendly products that are effective and safe for your vehicle and the environment.

What is the difference between waxing and ceramic coating?

Waxing provides a temporary shine and protective layer, while ceramic coating offers a longer-lasting, more durable protection that bonds with the paintwork.

How do I prepare my vehicle for detailing?

Please remove all personal items from your vehicle prior to the detailing appointment to ensure a thorough and efficient service.

What is paint correction?

Paint correction is a process that removes surface imperfections such as swirl marks, scratches, and oxidation from the vehicle's paint, resulting in a flawless finish.

Can you detail any type of vehicle?

Yes, we are equipped to detail a wide range of vehicles, including cars, SUVs, trucks, and vans.

Do you offer any packages or memberships for regular customers?

Yes, we offer several packages and membership plans that provide value for our repeat customers. These can include multi-service discounts and priority scheduling. Get in touch with us to learn more.

Is there anything I should do after my car is detailed?

To prolong the effects of the detailing, we recommend avoiding driving on dirt roads and not washing your car for at least 24 hours after wax or sealant applications.
